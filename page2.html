GENDERLESS SERIES

Everything in this world is divided by the terms of gender. Some actions or perceptions seem right depending on whether one thinks throughout the mindset of a woman or a man. I think that’s wrong. I think that one should think with the attitude of a person, not a gender. By separating men and women, and thus by separating two ways of thinking, you create a restriction on the number of possibilities that you could obtain. Everything is labeled that way; men who fall in love with men are gay, women that fall in love with women are called lesbians, while actually, we’re all just people who fall in love with other people. Everyone has their own personality next to their gender. In this series of portraits that is what I’m aiming for, people who have such strong personalities, that their gender becomes irrelevant.

No Link

No Link is a video art film about manipulated communication. It shows 13 performers expressing a personal, temporary state of mind. The performers were shot individually. However they are linked by editing manipulations creating a new unforeseen general tension which sometimes heavily contrasts or is surprisingly complementary to the individual original act. The conjunction of the different frames of mind creates an alienating, disaffecting perception of the conduct and demeanour of the protagonists/performers.

Written and Directed by Clara-Lane Lens

Actors (in order of appearance): Kjell Bracke, Quentin Remy, Thomas Claus, Camille Anthierens, Christiane van der Steen, Patricio Lagos, Rita O Bourgeois, Ian Ghysels, Dirk Desmedt, Michael Tasiaux, Isabelle Desprechins, Spencer Bogaert, Laurenzo Vergeynst
